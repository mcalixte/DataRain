Sensors Visualization Tech Challenge by QI

Challenge Title
Sensors Visualization Tech Challenge
Prize
500$ CAD Prize Value
Short Description
As sensors will play a big role in the Avatar Solution, teams need to think about how they can visualize the information to the operator in a way that is easy to understand and visually appealing. In this challenge you will be receiving temperature and precipitation data and will need to build an HTML visualization to representation. Bonus points if you can build it in a VR environment
Max Team Size
3
Overview:
What your challenge is about?
Making data gathering relevant and engaging for any kind of user
Why it is important?
IoT sensors do a great job at gathering data, but they are not useful if the data is not sorted and made available to the public in an easily understandable format. Also, a bit of creativity and design goes a long way into convincing people and make them understand the power of data.
What breakthrough do you want to achieve?
Make people aware of the air quality downtown and in the Quartier de l'Innovation area (Griffintown and Little Burgundy)
Guidelines:
Submission requirements: Prize(s): 500$ CAD Rules: Visualize the data in an HMTL frame. Use any visualization framework you are interested in. Get creative!
Bonus points if you can bring the visualisation to a VR space (You can use WebVR for example) and link the weather station real time through a TCP server (Hercules Setup Utility)
https://www.hw-group.com/software/hercules-setup-utility
Eligibility: Open to anyone Timeline: 24h
Judging criteria: Visually appealing, easy to integrate to any website, gives useful data, integrates data from other sensors or relevant sources, builds a complete environmental solution.
